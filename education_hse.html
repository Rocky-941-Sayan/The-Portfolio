High Secondary Education

I have completed my higher secondary education from New Andul Higher Class School, locally known as Andul School.


About Institute

It is located in West Bengal, India, specifically situated at Andul in Howrah district. The school is a bengali medium school. The school is for only Boys and it doesn't have an attached pre-primary section.


My journey

I wasn't a good student so first of all my admission for desired stream was not easy. Actually very few schools are here in my town where we need to give entry exam for admission. Finally I got admission in a school with my desired stream which was pure Science with biology.


But here was a cache. I wanted to take computer instead of biology. But again my parent was strictly conviced by one of my 10th class tutor. Actually I was already learning biology three months earlier from the admission. But vision was clear to do something related to computer. Then I also became confused what path to choose. But however I started science with biology.


But I lost somehow in mid of the session. All the subjects I had those were giving so much stress to me. I took private tutor for each subject. Then I slowly realise all these subjects I was studying kind of in a wrong way. I concluded that I need more of practical based learning instead of just memorizing theories and other stuff from books. So I used to invest all my capabilities of learning to a particular thing at a time without knowing how much time left to complete the syllabus.


I liked the mathematics little much and kept practicing, solving in various ways. But the tragedy is in final exam this subject betrayed me the most. Anyway I enjoyed in those school days.
