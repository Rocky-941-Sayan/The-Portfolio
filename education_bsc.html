About Institute

It is an undergraduate college in West Bengal, India, specifically situated at Andul in Howrah district. The college is affiliated with the University of Calcutta.


My journey

My graduation BSc was about 4 years. The COVID pandemic was running. I remember the first lockdown was just ended then the session started in online mode from august I think. After High-secondary I wasn't ready to do Bsc. I was very fond of editing at that time so I was thinking about choosing Animation as stream. But already The 1st broadwave of COVID had arrived in our country, so the lockdown was set to take place by govt. I also communicated with an company, named Hi-Tech and applied for the 2 year course. But my parent wasn't ready to permit me doing the course because it was an offline course and the lockdown event was taking place within three days of applying.


However I took unwillingly BSc Computer Science Hons. But then somehow I became happy about to learn many utilizations of computer. But It was so tough for me to understand and memorize all those things. Because I didn't have computer setup in home so I wasn't able to practice. I swear it was really tough for me to memorize all coding syntaxes, logics and fundamental things. So you can say I actually started learning or got interested to it very late. After one and a half of year I got my computer setup. My father bought that with great difficulty and I started the actually practice and after few months I gained the interest.


The college does not have on-campusing drive facility and also there was no internship facility. So I had to plan something for career myself. My initial plan was to finish the graduation completely without taking preparation of anyother thing. But no guidence at that time actually helped so much. I was doing straight, just learning the subjects without planning.


During BSc I did join an online 7-days learning bootcamp from DevTown.org. There I made clone of instagram and got certification. They gave a Google Developers Student club certificate also.
