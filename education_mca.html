My journey

My post-graduation MCA was about 2 years. Probably my biggest nightmare was happened during this. First of all I wasn't ready to do the post-graduation. Before taking this course my planning was to do external courses based on my skills learned so far and find/apply for jobs related to my stream. But my parent strictly wanted me to complete master degree afterthen I could do whatever I want to do. And somehow I also convinced and done this course.


Seriously speaking now I regret why I couldn't choose my instinct over this. I didn't learn any new thing or skill during these 2 years. My BSc(Comp.Sc) syllabus and MCA syllabus were very similar. That's why nothing new skill I learned related to computer background from my Institute. I thought it would be precious but it doesn't change anything. I learned new skills myself externally to enhance my skillset.


Then in the important time of applying in oncampus drives I faced a major bike accident, I am not even driving and got hospitalized I became broken cause I thought that I had to loss a year but somehow I was consoling myself. Then after not one month passed, within just 2 weeks I had chicken pox. You can't even imagine what was my mental state. I was bedridden for about two months. My parent also became broken. This is the nightmare I mentioned earlier.


I remember I just sat only accenture campus drive but couldn't clear. I regret every single moment about these two years untill I get a desired job. I applied for TCS NQT 2024-25 but one of my hospital checkup date issued on the same date.


During MCA I did an online internship from Plasmid Innovation. I made two short web-development projects and got certification. Besides this I have done two online platform courses, one from Coursera about Exploratory Data Analysis related to AI-ML and another from Udemy about Business Analytics.
