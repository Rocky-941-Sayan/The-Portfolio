Secondary Education

I have completed my secondary education from Sibpur Srimat Swami Prajnanananda Saraswati Vidyalaya (SSPS). It is located in West Bengal, India, specifically situated at Sibpur in Howrah district. The school is a Bengali-medium, Government-sponsored, higher secondary school. The school is for only Boys and it have an attached pre-primary section.


My journey

From 5th class to 10th class this was a long journey. I just remember that I was a good student in class but not according to exam results because those were very bad. I used to study hard, give good explanation on things, do homeworks perfectly. But I didn't rely on books exactly. I would just make my own explanation on study materials. Few teachers liked, many didn't like.


I would suffer a lot in fever in childhood. Those sufferings hampered my study a lot. As a effect I couldn't give my best in many of the exams. I don't remember exactly but I wasn't able to give few exams also. By the medical terminologies I had passed very few exam by the teachers. I also failed in some cases. Most of the time my answers didn't satisfy the teachers.


At kindergarten / primary school I stood between 1 and 10. two times I remember I became first in the class also. But in secondary school first year I fell in high fever, actually I had typhoid that time. almost 2-3 months spoiled. Unfortunately that's how my journey started.


During those time I liked to paint. I was interested to singing, dancing also. I would participate in the talent exams. I remembered I became 1st once. And after all these I was a bad student unfortunately.
